TCS Selection Procedure and written test pattern

The selection procedure of TCS consists of following 3 rounds
1. Written Test- (aptitude, reasoning, verbal ability)
2. Technical Interview
3. HR Interview

TCS Written Test Pattern
No of questions 35
Time Limit 80 minutes
Negative Mark : 1/3

Some points to concentrate
♦ For each wrong questions 0.33 marks will be deducted
♦ Take care of negative marking and attempt a question only if you are sure
♦ Lots of unnecessary data (read carefully)
♦ Practice previous years questions and mock on line tests, it will help you a lot
